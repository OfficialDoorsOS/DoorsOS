What Is DoorsOS?

DoorsOS is a Open-Source Operating system centered around Maximum Customizability

We only install what you need, allowing you to install any packages and alter the system in any way you want.

  • We have two different downloadable versions:
  • Open Doors: This is the complete and up to date version of doors; its plug and play. (recomended for normies).
  • Closed Doors: This is "bare" doors and only contains the required programs and dependancies. (only for people who REALLY understand linux).
